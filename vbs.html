Fort Spring Missionary Baptist Church

Vacation Bible School

July 8 - July 12, 5:30pm - 8:45pm

We'll take kids deep into an amazing undersea adventure where they’ll experience the ever-flowing, never-ending love of God. During Scuba VBS at Fort Spring Missionary Baptist Church, kids will be immersed in the Word and discover what living water is really all about!

What do kids do at VBS? Kids receive a small meal, participate in memorable Bible-learning activities, sing fun songs, play teamwork-building games, experience one-of-a-kind Bible adventures, collect Bible Memory Buddies to remind them of God’s love, and test out sciency-fun experiments that get imagination bubbling. Plus, kids will learn to look for evidence of God all around them through something called God Sightings. Each day concludes with a Fin-tastic Finale that gets everyone (including family and friends!) involved in living what they’ve learned.

Who can come to VBS? VBS is for kids up to and including those entering the 12th grade. We will not have a nursery, so kids under 4 years of age must be accompanied by an adult.
