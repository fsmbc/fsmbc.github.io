Fort Spring Missionary Baptist Church

Mission Statement

Our mission at Fort Spring Missionary Baptist Church is to simply teach and preach the gospel of Jesus Christ.

We believe that the mission of the church is to produce other Christians

We strive to plant the seed of God's word and allow His Spirit to work in the hearts of those who hear

We believe that the future of God's earthly church relies heavily upon the development and nurturing of our young people

We believe that salvation is a free gift from God through faith in Jesus Christ
