Fort Spring Missionary Baptist Church

Ladies' Ministry

Looking for a place to relax, unwind, laugh and connect with other ladies? Our Ladies Ministry strives to provide opportunities that enable ladies not only to find the fellowship they need, but also to enjoy a haven from hectic schedules and to draw closer to God and sisters in Christ.

When does the Ladies' Ministry meet?

Our Ladies Ministry meets on the first Thursday of each month at 6:30pm in the basement of the church.

Our meetings are a time to build and strengthen friendships, encourage one another, share devotions and to grow together in Christ.

What does the Ladies' Ministry do?

Along with our monthly meetings we also provide various opportunities to reach out to those in our church and community throughout the year. We have special events for ladies to come together to be refreshed and renewed with sisters in Christ.

Please join us!

In our Ladies Ministry you will find a place you belong, to be encouraged and strengthened, and to be cared for among a warm and friendly group of Ladies who love the Lord and have a desire to serve Him and others.

Our prayer is that God will be glorified through this ministry and that it will be of service and value to you in your Christian walk.

Come and be a part of what God is doing in and through the Ladies Ministry.
